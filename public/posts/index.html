Factorio

In the realm of gears and steam, Where conveyor belts weave like a dream, Factorio, the land of machination, A world born from imagination. Iron and copper, ores of the earth, Fuel the factories, giving them birth. Belts intertwine in a rhythmic dance, Engines hum in a hypnotic trance. Pipes hiss with the steam’s hot breath, In this symphony of progress and death. Automatons toil under the sun and moon, To the rhythm of the industrial tune.
